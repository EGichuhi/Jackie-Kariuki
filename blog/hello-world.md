This year’s International Women’s Day was truly special for our Kenyan community here in Toronto. It was more than just an event, it was a beautiful reminder of the strength, resilience, and unity of the women who continue to shape and uplift our community every day.

From the moment we gathered, there was a sense of joy and connection in the room. Seeing women of all ages come together, sharing stories, laughter, and encouragement—was incredibly inspiring. The energy was warm, welcoming, and full of pride in who we are and where we come from.

We took time to recognize and celebrate the achievements of Kenyan women, both here in Canada and back home. Whether through family, career, or community work, the impact is undeniable. It was also a chance to reflect on the journey ahead and the importance of continuing to support and empower one another.

A heartfelt thank you to everyone who attended, contributed, and helped make the day so memorable. Your presence and spirit are what make our community so strong.

As you look through the photos, we hope you can feel the joy and connection that made this day so meaningful.

Together, we continue to rise. 💛
