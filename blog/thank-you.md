This June, Women of the Word will be gathering for a special retreat designed to create space for rest, reflection, and spiritual growth. It’s an opportunity to step away from the busyness of everyday life and reconnect, with God, with ourselves, and with one another.

Through meaningful conversations, shared moments, and time in the Word, the retreat will be a chance to be encouraged, uplifted, and strengthened in faith. Whether you’re coming to recharge, seek clarity, or simply be in community, this time together promises to be both peaceful and powerful.

We look forward to sharing this experience and growing together. 💛
